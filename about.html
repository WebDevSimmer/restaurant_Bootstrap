About Us

Welcome to Chez Victor, a restaurant with a unique and flavorful history. Our story begins with our founder, a Chinese immigrant with a passion for cooking and a dream to share his delicious cuisine with the world. With his culinary skills and dedication, our founder Victor established Chez Victor as a destination for authentic Chinese cuisine. Over time, our restaurant has grown into a beloved community gathering place, welcoming guests from all walks of life to indulge in our delectable dishes.

At Chez Victor, we pride ourselves on using only the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking techniques to create dishes that are both comforting and flavorful. Our menu boasts a variety of classic Chinese dishes, as well as a few surprises that are sure to tantalize your taste buds.
